chromaform

A prog-metal band. Jazz fusion, deathcore, and orchestral music woven into one sound. Formed in 2023 by Christian Schreil, Andreas Grimell, and me.

Between us: previous work in Vildhjarta, Uneven Structure, and Means End. The sound we're after with Chromaform is further out than any of those — more polyrhythmic, more harmonic, less constrained by subgenre.

// the album — in nihilum

Sixteen tracks, grouped into four parts. The album is the affective half of the In Nihilum project — each track sits alongside a chapter of the book, carrying the same idea in a different register. Same argument, louder grammar.
