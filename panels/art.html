// the house

I designed and built my house at Stenkroksvägen over three years. It was featured on Grand Designs Sweden and won Gold and Silver at the ADC International Architecture & Design Awards 2023. The project management was harder than the engineering. I'd do it again.

// sandvik — the statue

A monumental chrome sculpture commissioned by Sandvik. Mirror-polished stainless steel, robed allegorical figure holding a golden orb, staged in an industrial hangar under diffused light. The brief was classical; the execution is unapologetic.
